Our Why

Empowering everyone to have a voice in their vision for the future

  • Our Mission Statement

    The choices we make today have a direct impact on the world of tomorrow. At kindbox.com, we aim to empower everyone to have a voice in their vision for the future. We all have the ability to make meaningful global change and build the world we want to live in.

  • What is the problem?

    We believe that consumers should be able to make ethical and sustainable choices each and every time they shop. Choices that not only protect the Earth, but make it a better place to live for everyone. The disconnect between our consumer choices and the impact that these choices have on the world has never been so distant. Kindbox.com is here to solve that problem.

  • How do we fix it?

    Each and every product purchased from our platform has the intention of making the world a better place. As we say, every product is here for a reason! Heavily aligned with the SDGs for the world, our products aim to help the community to work towards a better future for all. As a kindbox customer, you will be directing parts of the profits to projects that matter to you. Whether itâ€™s a new Library in Nepal or a chimpanzee enclosure in Borneo, each purchase you make gives you the power to change the world for the better.

  • What are the results?

    With community projects being sourced around the world on an ongoing basis, Kindbox gives you the power to make a meaningful impact on those who need it most. Whilst simultaneously buying ethical and sustainably sourced items, you will not only improve the lives of others but yours too. Kindbox.com is the place where you can shop with kindness.
